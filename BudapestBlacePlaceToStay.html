Castle District

Restaurants and cafes here are in abundance, so it is easy to find a place to dine in or just grab a bite. Besides, the fine sites of the city also lie in the close proximity of this district. But the heart stealer is the breathtaking panorama of the River Danube and the chain bridge, so for those ideal evenings with a cup of coffee in your hand, find a place to stay in the Castle District!
