Best Places to Party in Ibiza

Best Day Trips from Ibiza

Ibiza, honoree of UNESCO’s World Heritage site distinction, is a Mediterranean paradise and a million islands in one. Beaches, open-air sports, sunlit twilights and a special sort of magic, Ibiza is a synonym to ‘party till you drop’. Having made a name for itself, Ibizian nightlife is celebrated worldwide. Innovative clubs, creative street theatre, island discotheques; is all a part of its irresistible nighttime diversion. From the best nightclubs in Ibiza to more intimate venues, if clubbing is one of your favourite things to do, Ibiza has a great range of night spots. Whether you're after a hardcore night out or something more laid back, you'll find it in one of these top three places to party in Ibiza:

1. Ibiza Town

Located on the southeast coast of the island of Ibiza, it is truly the reflection of the magic and beauty of the island. From spectacular moorings and cobbled paths of the old town to hippie markets and waterfronts bustling with boozers, Ibiza Town indeed has it all. Most celebrated clubs of the island, like Pacha, Heart and Lío, are right on its doorstep. Furthermore, it has plethora of shops, boutiques, restaurants and bars.

2. Playa d'en Bossa

If you are up for all-day and all-night action, Playa d'en Bossa caters for just about everything- clubs, bars, biergartens, bands, impromptu beach drinking sessions, after-parties – literally, everything. It is lined with endless shops, restaurants and bars. Ibiza Rocks Diner, Tantra, Lips and Dunes are particularly popular. Playa d'en Bossa is the main island destination for party-going tourists of all ages. It is located in immediate proximity to some of the popular clubs, including Ushuaïa, Hï Ibiza, Sankeys and Swag Ibiza, among others. It has excellent connectivity via buses and taxis, with Ibiza Town and other major resorts and is barely 5 kilometers from the airport.

3. Sant Antoni de Portmany

The picturesque harbor, pine woods and fine sandy beaches is a gimmick to attract tourists from around the world to this town on the western coast of Ibiza. It hosts a variety of bars ranging from house music to rock and indie pop. You might as well like to spend your evening at the Sunset Strip, listening to sublime chill-out tunes from top DJs, along the coast of the natural bay of Sant Antoni de Portmany. How about having your first dance in a pre-club bar and then partying the night away in one of the town's super clubs, like Es Paradis or Eden? Dine, chill and dance with views of the dockyard and bay. From poolside luxury and private parties to sports and entertainment bars, San Antonio has a slice of everything a party-freak would ask for.

You have to get out and about to get the most out of this ‘clubbing capital of the universe’.
