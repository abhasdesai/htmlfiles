Best Place to Stay in Ibiza

Ibiza is a paradise for walking, riding and swimming in clear seas. It is considered to be a popular tourist destination, especially due to its legendary and at times riotous nightclub based nightlife. Though primarily known for its modish lifestyle and hippie party scenes, Ibiza has much more to offer than just clubs and rave parties. Set on an excursion and discover the hidden Ibiza of provincial towns, farmhouses, sandy beaches and rocky bays in the back of beyond. While planning a vacay to the clubbing capital of the universe, choose where to stay in Ibiza, depending on what you seek, from the top 3 places for accommodation for a tourist here:

1. Ibiza Town

Ibiza Town is the capital and the largest city on the island and really does have it all. The sheer diversity that this city offers is exceptional. A shopper's paradise awaits you in Ibiza Town, with a great selection of small boutiques and high-end fashion stores. The port area with its buzzing nightlife, the historic old town and clubs like Pacha make this town so popular among the international crowd. A really cheap and fun way to stroll around the island is by joining the regular ferries that will take you from Ibiza port to the beautiful sandy beaches of Talamanca, Figueretas and beyond.

2. Sant Antoni de Portmany

This town, located on the west coast of Ibiza, is known for its lively nightlife, booze-ups and vast natural harbor, Sant Antoni’s Bay. This Balearic town has an interesting historic quarter and a mellow side along the beautiful, crystal-clear waters and the cafe-lined sunset strip. The variety of accommodations and leisure activities it offers make it one of the most attractive tourist destinations in the island.

3. Santa Eulària des Riu

Santa Eulalia is the "other side" of Ibiza, lush greenery and unpretentious, heavenly serenity a world away from the frenetic beat of Ibiza Town. This beautiful town on the East coast of the island, is popular with families and those looking for a quieter holiday. It has a long-established reputation as the island's gastronomic and cultural centre, with some of the island's best restaurants and art galleries. A couple of family run hotels, with lush tropical gardens, clean pools, the beautiful and exclusive Yacht Marina ensure that life in Santa Eulalia is led at a rather slower and more relaxing pace..

So choose the best place to stay in Ibiza depending on your preference and experience all the best bits of this stylish Balearic island. Buen viaje!
