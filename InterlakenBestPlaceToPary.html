Best Places to Party in Interlaken

Best Day Trips from Interlaken

Interlaken- definitely not the city that takes to bed early! Besides the impeccable mountains and valleys to gawk at in the daytime, Interlaken also offers a lot of thriving and engaging nightlife. Once you are done strolling leisurely by the lakeside or repelling off a 5000ft. cliff, come to life along with the city at one of the many nightclubs in Interlaken. These top 3 places to party in Interlaken are enough to keep you up until wee hours in the morning. So plan your itinerary keeping these pubs, clubs and grubs in mind:

1. Hoeheweg

This chirpy boulevard is the focal point of umpteen travelers staying in hostels and resorts stationed in this very neighborhood. Stretching all the way from Interlaken-West to Interlaken-Ost, Hoeheweg boasts many vibrant hotels, resorts, hostels, boutiques, souvenir shops, lush gardens and abundant nightlife. Hohematte is located in the heart of this neighborhood in Interlaken, with the vistas opening up from the flowering meadows to mountains in the backdrop, beyond to the epic views of Jungfrau. Pass by the horse-drawn carriages before sundown and grab a scrumptious meal at Johnny’s Pub and Grill after dark. Old Fashion Bar and Taverne are popular bars in Interlaken if you plan to get high.

2. Rosenstrasse

This very popular street in Interlaken is replete with a lively night scene. Being a popular neighborhood among tourists for its close proximity to main travel attractions makes Rosenstrasse a hot district to party in Interlaken. Crazy Mary Pub is always brimming with locals and travelers seeking a fun night out in Interlaken. Then there is Sandwich Bar to offer you good food and great booze as you set yourself in party mode. Brasserie 17 has a pretty little summer terrace, delicious snacks and beer and soothing live music. If you are too much of a liquor aficionado, try the diverse Kirsch, the cool Rugenbrau or the sharp Alpenbitter- some of the most famous locals brews of Interlaken.

2. Hauptstrasse

This eminent street hosts two of the most frequented nightlife spots in Interlaken. Known for its cleanliness and spick ‘n’ span locality, Hauptstrasse offers many great accommodations and party zones. Balmer’s Hostel, besides being a haven for travelers, offers a decent nightclub and a basement bar. Funny Farm is another great chill-out corner set up in outdoors. Just bouncing back and forth in these two bars in Interlaken would be enough to rock your night.

If you have a cultural edge to your personality, then summer months would be ideal for your Interlaken visit. Catch ‘William Tell’ being performed at Tellspiele Theater by Friedrich Schiller.
