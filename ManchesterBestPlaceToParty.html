Best Places To Party in Manchester

With a large chunk of its population dominated by the young generation, to expect the nights to be quiet is a bit too much to ask from Manchester. With places that will give you a distance from the crowds to the places that will put you in the central location, Manchester is one of the most diverse cities in the world to be in. Frequently referred to as one of the coolest cities in UK, Manchester is a crammed up collection of some of the most hip neighbourhoods in the region. Now given the choice in some of the greatest place, one tends to get confused. So to help you choose the best from great, here is the list of the best neighbourhoods to party in Manchester:

1. Deansgate

Nestled in the heart of the city centre of Manchester, Deansgate is naturally a vibrant area. Playing home to hordes of youth here, this place obviously has easy access to some of the best of the city's bars, clubs and boutiques. A prime location, Deansgate is well famed for both its accommodation and its nightlife. Some of the most famous bars in the location include Cloud 23, The Milton Club, The Knott, Revolution Manchester, The Liquor Store, Lock 91, The Gas Lamp, Be at One Manchester and The Lost Dene. Other well flocked pubs and nightclubs include Atlas Bar, The Moon under Water, Mulligans, the Botanist Manchester and 42's.

2. The Northern Quarter

Snuggled up between the Arndale Centra and the Piccadilly train station, The Northern Quarter is a place consisting of a labyrinth of small streets and alleyways brimming up with independent, cafes and boutiques. This is the place you go if what you are searching for includes the best coffee in the world, a pint of the locally brewed ale or the famous vintage grams. Another one of the attracting factors is the beautiful architecture of the place. The best bars in the region include The Whiskey Jar, Dive NQ, Kosmonaut, Dusk Till Pawn, Lost in Tokyo, Hold Fast Bar, Apotheca, Bar 21, the Pen and Pencil, Night & Day Cafe, Guilty by Association and the Walrus Manchester. The best pubs and nightclubs include The Smithfield Market Tavern, the English Lounge and Mint Lounge.

3. Ancoats

If a neighbourhood has the title of being one of the hippest neighbourhoods in the whole of UK, you can easily expect the entire place will be buzzing with energy. Located at just a hops distance from the Northern Quarter, though Ancoats is still building up its premises, it is already a hit in the region. Brimming with coffee shops, independent theatre spaces popping up on weekly basis, bars, pubs and nightclubs, the place is sure to give you an experience that cannot be matched by many. Some of the most famous bars in the region include Seven Brothers Beerhouse, The Crown & Kettle, The Ancoats Lad, Bar Fringe, Elnecot, The Marble Arch, Hula, Manchester Bierkeller, Panacea Bar and Jimmy's.
