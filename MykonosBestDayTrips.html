Best Day trips from Mykonos

A complete high-season mix of hedonistic holidaymakers, Mykonos is the perfect party hub of Greece. Known for its eclectic, cosmopolitan atmosphere, exciting nightlife and the beautiful Cycladic architecture along with spectacular beaches, this atoll manages to make your travel experience a magnificent one. Catering to a wide age group, Mykonos is full of activities for young and old. A much frequented mecca for the luxury lovers, you can stay in exquisite hotels and swim in the exotic beaches. Centrally located, it curbs your need to explore other islands and the vicinities. But for an extensive and complete Greek experience, you need to widen your horizons. So here are the best day trips you can take from Mykonos:

1. Amorgos

Considered the most beautiful island in Greece, Amorgos is a wonder in natural graces. Blessed with spectacular terrains, traditional architecture and fabulous beaches, this island is located near Naxos and Ios. One of the greatest tourist attractions in the vicinity is the monastery of Hozoviotissa perched on sharp slopes of rock giving a breathtaking sight of the sea. A place for the hikers, Amorgos offers you more than a hundred opportunities to be awed. With a number of ferry companies operating there is a possibility of taking a ferry to and fro from Amorgos, one way costing a little over 2 hours.

2. Ios

Famous for its bewitching beaches, Ios is one of the must visit destinations in the list. Attracting mainly the young crowd, the place is brimming with activity through the day right into the night. With the parties starting in the midday, you can literally lose yourself on mesmerising tunes and beautiful sights. Besides unprecedented revelling, the place also proves as a perfect hideout to relax on the wonderful beaches with soft sands and pristine, emerald waters. There are many ferry services available for the commutation taking 2 hours of travel time.

3. Santorini

A precious gen of the Aegean, Santorini is actually a collection of islands including Thíra, Thirassiá, Asproníssi, Palea and Nea Kaméni, all located in the southernmost part of Cyclades. Complete with multicoloured cliffs rising up from a sea drowned caldera, Santorini appears to be a one whole white and blue castle with countless storeys. A confluence of history and futuristic endowments, Santorini offers you a peek into the past while effortlessly gliding into the future, giving way to impeccable artists, excellent wineries, a unique microbrewery and some of the best lodging and dining experiences the Cyclades can showcase. And besides all this, a cherry on top is the multicoloured, immensely gorgeous beaches. Another one of the island getaways, the place is approachable through the ferry services available by various companies taking a beautiful journey of 2 hours and 40minutes.

4. Naxos

Holding great mythological reference, Naxos is fabled to be the nuptial isle of the god Dionysus, the God of Wine. The largest island in Cyclades, Naxos is located in the centre of Paros and Mykonos. This place is ideal for the family trips or romantic getaways where travellers seek nothing beyond tranquil, serene and exotic beaches. Some of the most prestigious collection of beaches includes Plaka, Agios Prokopios, Orkos and Mikri Vigla. This place is also an option for the adrenaline junkies with the availability of activities such as windsurfing and kite-surfing. Complete with 7 sailings a week, the average sailing time for the ferry is 1 hour 15 minutes.

5. Andros

One of the closest Greek islands to Athens, Andros is a vastly popular weekend destination for both tourists and Athenians. A beautiful attraction with rich historical beauty, Andors boasts a distinguished elegant architecture of Chora, complete with green landscape of the inland and the fabulous beaches. Some of the most beautiful beaches are Ahla, Vitali and Vlychada; however they are accessible only by bikes or by foot. Along with this, this boasts interesting art museums hosting frequent world exhibitions. Running as frequent as 12 times a week, you can easily plan a day trip as the beautiful journey only takes 2h 20mins.
