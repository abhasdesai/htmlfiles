Best Places to Party in Prague

Prague is one city that takes its liquid refreshment and after dark entertainment way too seriously. Already known as the beer hotspot, the name is justified by the marvellous beer garden, a new trend of cocktail bars which had taken the entire world by a storm. Other than the obvious, the city is famous for the plethora of music and dancing options for the inhabitants and tourists. However, contrary to the original belief, the best place to find the fabled nightlife of the city is in mysterious Old Town instead of the small city centre. Here is a rundown of the top 3 places to party in Prague of your choice whether it includes music, booze or the general merriment, this place has it all.

1. Old Town Square

This is one of those all encompassing places that hold a variety of revelling options for the people to choose from. Profusion of options in the Cocktail Bar include Tretter's Bar, Zapa Bar and Tynska bar and Books, each holding its own case to visit the place. If the night you're looking for includes dancing,Old Town is the ideal place to visit, housing a wide variety of discotheques including Radost FX, Roxy, Karlovy Lazne, Lucerna Music Clubs and M1 Secret Lounge quenching your music appetite perfectly. For a more subtle taste one can visit U Maleho Glena, Ungelt Jazz and Blues club or the Reduta Jazz Club.

2. Nove Mesto

Despite having a shrimp size, Nove Mesto doesn't necessarily imply a complete paucity of nightlife. Though comparatively new and still in a struggling infrastructural stage, this place has a lot to offer to the after dark entertainment sector in all fields including music, liquor and food. Some of the leading choice for the bars, pubs and clubs include Bohemian Bar, Prague Golf and Games, Prague Pub Crawl, Drunken Monkey Pub and the Backpacker Pubcrawl. For the music junkies, Mesto has bars like the Lucrena Music Bar, Parlour, La Loca Music Bar and Lounge and Be Bop with live bands and recorded EDM playing almost every day.

3. Hradcany & Letna

Built a long while after both the new town and the old town, this particular district of Prague has something that no other in the world can offer. Hradcany and Letna is almost entirely made up of the Prague castle, adding an element of historical significance to your nightlife in Prague. A magnificent regal view coupled with your favourite cocktails in hand- what more can you ask for from your night? Some of the most famous pubs and bars in the place include, Stredoveka Krcma, The loop Jazz club, Valasska and Snack Annonce in addition to many colloquial taverns.

So now you have the list of the places providing the best nightlife in the region. What is left is just have to go and experience! So plan your itinerary now and party royalty style!
