Best Places to Party in Split

Best Day Trips from Split

Energy, razzle-dazzle and glitz- nightlife in Split, the second largest city of Croatia, is all about fun. From sundown to sunup, indulge yourself in a wide array of bars, pubs, dance clubs and lounges here. This buzzing seafront promenade has as many places to boast in the daytime as after dusk. Split locals are bar-lovers to their very core. But Split nightlife boasts more quality than quantity. So check out these top 3 places to party in Split:

1. Riva

Many bars and cafes line up opposite to the Diocletian Palace in the famous Split waterfront- Riva. It is the perfect place to warm yourself up for a late night out in Split. The most popular scene here is The Ghetto Club, located in a courtyard on the Dosud Street. Its live music performances and art shows are widely popular among travelers. A few steps away is the epic Puls Bar, a two storey restaurant cum bar that offers great food, fancy cocktails and an excellent House Wine. After you are done with food and drinks, get inside the palace to explore a bunch of other places like Gaga, Red Room, Luxor to name a few.

2. Poljud

The area behind Poljud Stadium comes to life mainly after 1am, when everyone else is fast asleep except the real party animals. A 20 minute walk or a 5 minute Taxi ride from the Old Town gets you here, where Croatians and travelers get along for dance and booze. Vanilla Club, a place packed to capacity almost all the time in summers, offers a huge terrace to find a corner somewhere and people-watch. Hemingway Bar, another crowd-pleasing nightspot in Split is perched right next to the Azure Sea. Gorgeous interiors with a beautiful dance floor, plenty of space and gigantic VIP lounges invited international bands and DJs from across lands.

3. Bacvice Beach

Bacvice is a complex of bars, restaurants and cafes to let you spend the entire evening and night here. This area is up and running in the late hours, since most of the Old Town in Split switches off lights around 11-12pm. Neighborhood to the left end of Bacvice beach turns into a Party Mile with all the buildings switching their avatar of a great nigh out. Audience here is mostly young and super energetic. Tropic Club, the most popular one here, turns the radar high after midnight.
