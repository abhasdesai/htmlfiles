Best Place to Stay in St. Petersburg

A city harbouring revolutionary spirit, the sheer grandeur and history of Russia's imperial capital never ceases to amaze the witnesses. Initially intended to play an exhibit to show Russia's growing status to the world today, St Petersburg still feels every bit of regalia with its historic heart largely frozen in time. Known as the Venice of the North, this city has a wide network of elegant 342 bridges. Be it history or art, this is one place pioneer in art, possessing almost unrivalled treasure trove of art and culture. So a visit to this city overflowing with art festivals demands an accommodation to match the style and standard of the place you’re going to witness. Here is the list of the top 3 places for accommodation for a tourist in St. Petersburg:

1. Admiralteisky

The residential address of the Russian Emperor, this is one place where you can simply spend the day strolling and still not be able to get enough. Complete with St. Isaac’s Cathedral State Museum and the Winter Palace of Peter, this is one neighbourhood in St. Petersburg that reminds you of history with every step you take. For those who spend the day basking in the warmth of history, the selection of food is beyond exquisite with a plethora of cafes and restaurants well established in the midst. With a wide selection of hotels, this is one of the most sought after places to stay in St. Petersburg.

2. Liteyny

Right in the centre of the city, this is one of the places with potential location and peaceful environment. Walking in this neighbourhood is like being transported to a different world full of beautiful sights and wonderful arts. From the Anna Akhmatova Museum at Fountain House till the Applied Arts Museums, this is the focal point of museums of art and history, making it to be a paradise for culture enthusiasts. Integrated in the veins are several soulful cafes and restaurants. This is the place to visit if solitude and distance from civilization is what you want to witness.

3. Smolny

Another centrally located one, Smolny is a compound of historically interrelated buildings erected in 18th and 19th centuries. This wide spaced and beautifully planned neighbourhood is a focal point of parks and cathedrals. Some of the most famous structures in the neighbourhood include Smolny Cathedral, Tauride Garden, Leningrad Centre and Palais de Tauride. As is evident, Smolny is known for its standard of living and is the home of many exquisite lounges and cafes right in the proximity of hotels. Along with having excellent connectivity to all things touristy, this area lets you base yourself in some of the best hotels in the region for self reflection and quiet escape while being under your budget.

So be ready to be blown away by the history of the place while making your stay memorial! St. Petersburg is all set to welcome you!
