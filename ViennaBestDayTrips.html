Best Day trips from Vienna

1. Budapest

Located at a distance of 240 kilometeres, Budapest is also nicknamed as the "Paris of East". Perched on a beautiful location astride the Danube River, Budapest, though a long drive from Vienna, is worth the pain complete with breathtaking architecture spanning styles from Baroque to Neoclassical, and Art Nouveau; and the chance to soak in the city's famous therapeutic springs. Some of the major attractions in the place are Castle hill, the Buda Castle, Fisherman's Bastion and the Matthias Church. 2. Salzburg

Counted as one of Europe's most admired medieval town, with architecture and natural endowments taking equal credits, Salzburg also enjoys a special fame in the world of music as the birthplace of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. Some of the most famous places in the region include the gorgeous Mirabell Palace and gardens, Hohensalzburg Castle; Mozart's Birthplace, Residenzplatz and the impressive marble fountain of Residenzbrunnen.One of the best vehicles for transport is the train taking 2.5- 3 hours of travel time. Other options include cabs and cars both costing 3 hours of journey each.
